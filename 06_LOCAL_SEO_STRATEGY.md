# Local SEO Strategy — Right Away Services LLC

## Objective

Build strong local organic relevance for handyman and home repair searches across Florida’s Space Coast, with the heaviest emphasis on **Palm Bay**, **Melbourne**, and **West Melbourne**.

## Geographic targeting model

### Tier 1 priority markets
These should receive the strongest optimization focus:
- Palm Bay, FL
- Melbourne, FL
- West Melbourne, FL

### Tier 2 nearby markets
These support wider Space Coast relevance:
- Malabar, FL
- Grant-Valkaria, FL
- Indialantic, FL
- Melbourne Beach, FL
- Satellite Beach, FL
- Indian Harbour Beach, FL
- Rockledge, FL
- Viera, FL
- Suntree, FL

## Local SEO structure

### 1. Core homepage optimization
Homepage should target broad terms like:
- handyman services Space Coast
- handyman Palm Bay
- handyman Melbourne
- home repairs Space Coast Florida

### 2. Service pages
Each service page should target:
- service keyword
- service + Palm Bay
- service + Melbourne
- service + Space Coast

Examples:
- drywall repair Palm Bay
- fixture installation Melbourne
- handyman for home repairs near Palm Bay

### 3. Area pages
Each city page should target:
- handyman + city
- home repair + city
- local handyman services + city

Examples:
- handyman Palm Bay FL
- handyman Melbourne FL
- home repair West Melbourne FL

### 4. Blog support content
Use blog posts to capture long-tail informational searches such as:
- how much does handyman work cost in Palm Bay
- common drywall repair problems in Florida humidity
- what repairs should landlords handle before renting a property in Melbourne FL

## On-page optimization framework

Each page should contain:
- unique title tag
- unique meta description
- H1 with localized relevance
- supporting H2s
- internal links
- CTA
- FAQ section when useful
- image alt text
- schema markup

## Local relevance signals to include

- service area references
- city names in naturally relevant sections
- nearby landmark / area mentions where appropriate
- service radius explanation
- local homeowner pain points:
  - humidity-related wear
  - weather exposure
  - rental turnover repairs
  - aging fixtures
  - punch-list work
  - move-in / move-out fixes

## Recommended title tag formulas

### Homepage
`Handyman Services in Palm Bay & Melbourne, FL | Right Away Services LLC`

### Service pages
`Drywall Repair in Palm Bay & Melbourne, FL | Right Away Services LLC`

### City pages
`Handyman Services in Palm Bay, FL | Right Away Services LLC`

### Blog
`How to Prepare Your Home for Small Repairs in Palm Bay, FL`

## Metadata rules

- keep title tags readable and non-spammy
- prioritize conversion and local clarity
- mention brand name
- avoid repeating same title format without variation

## Internal linking strategy

Every major service page should link to:
- Palm Bay area page
- Melbourne area page
- West Melbourne area page
- contact page

Every major city page should link to:
- handyman services page
- top 3–5 service pages
- contact page

## Trust and proximity signals

Site should include:
- service area list in footer
- contact details in footer
- local business wording throughout site
- map embed or service area map section
- review/testimonial snippets mentioning locations when available

## Schema requirements

- LocalBusiness
- FAQPage
- BreadcrumbList
- Service
- WebSite

- project photos uploaded consistently
- service descriptions aligned with website language
