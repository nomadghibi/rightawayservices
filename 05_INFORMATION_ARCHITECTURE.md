# Information Architecture — Right Away Services LLC

## Navigation structure

### Primary nav
- Home
- Services
- Service Areas
- About
- FAQ
- Blog
- Contact

### Header CTA
- Call Now
- Request Estimate

## Footer
- short company description
- key services
- key service areas
- contact details
- hours
- legal links
- social links if available

## URL strategy

### Core pages
- `/`
- `/about`
- `/services`
- `/service-areas`
- `/contact`
- `/faq`
- `/blog`
- `/privacy-policy`
- `/terms`

### Service page structure
- `/services/[slug]`

Example:
- `/services/drywall-repair`
- `/services/fixture-installation`

### Area page structure
- `/service-areas/[slug]`

Example:
- `/service-areas/palm-bay-fl`
- `/service-areas/melbourne-fl`

### Blog structure
- `/blog/[slug]`

## Recommended page hierarchy

### Home
Purpose:
- brand introduction
- trust
- overview of services
- lead generation

### Services overview
Purpose:
- list all main services
- route users to specific service pages

### Individual service pages
Purpose:
- rank for service-intent keywords
- explain work performed
- convert via CTA

### Service Areas overview
Purpose:
- show local footprint
- route users to city pages
- reinforce local service coverage

### Individual area pages
Purpose:
- rank for geo-intent searches
- localize service relevance
- link to related services

### About
Purpose:
- credibility
- trust
- business story
- why choose us

### Contact
Purpose:
- direct conversion
- call / form / email / hours

### FAQ
Purpose:
- answer objections
- support SEO with helpful content

### Blog
Purpose:
- long-tail SEO
- authority
- homeowner education

## Internal linking rules

### From home
Link to:
- top services
- priority city pages
- contact
- FAQ

### From service pages
Link to:
- related service pages
- top city pages
- contact

### From city pages
Link to:
- service pages relevant to that market
- nearby cities
- contact

### From blog posts
Link to:
- relevant service pages
- contact
- city pages where natural

## Recommended home page modules

1. Hero
2. Service trust highlights
3. Main service cards
4. Why choose us
5. Service areas
6. Before/after or project examples
7. Reviews
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA

## Template rules

Every service template should have:
- hero
- service intro
- common problems solved
- what’s included
- why choose us
- FAQs
- CTA

Every city template should have:
- hero
- local intro
- services offered in that area
- why locals choose the company
- FAQs
- nearby areas
- CTA

## Canonical rules
- use one canonical domain version only
- preferred recommendation: `https://rightawayservices.com`
- 301 redirect `www` to non-`www` or vice versa consistently
