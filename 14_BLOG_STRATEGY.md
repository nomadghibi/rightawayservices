# Blog Strategy — Right Away Services LLC

## Purpose

The blog should support local SEO by publishing useful homeowner content tied to handyman, home repair, and property maintenance topics relevant to Florida’s Space Coast.

## Blog goals

- capture long-tail search traffic
- support topical authority
- answer homeowner questions
- create internal links to service pages
- support local relevance for Palm Bay and Melbourne

## Content categories

### 1. Homeowner education
Examples:
- when to call a handyman vs contractor
- common small repairs homeowners ignore too long
- how to prepare for a handyman visit

### 2. Florida-specific maintenance
Examples:
- humidity-related drywall and paint issues
- seasonal home maintenance tips for Florida homeowners
- pre-storm small repair checklists

### 3. Local landlord / property manager content
Examples:
- rental turnover repair checklist in Melbourne FL
- punch-list repairs before a new tenant moves in
- fast fixes that improve rental presentation

### 4. Selling / buying prep
Examples:
- top small repairs to make before listing your home
- affordable handyman updates that improve curb appeal
- move-in repair checklist for new homeowners

## Starter blog post ideas

1. `Top Small Home Repairs Homeowners in Palm Bay Shouldn’t Ignore`
2. `Drywall, Paint, and Fixture Problems Caused by Florida Humidity`
3. `What to Fix Before Listing Your Home in Melbourne, FL`
4. `Handyman vs Contractor: Which One Do You Need for Small Repairs?`
5. `Rental Turnover Repair Checklist for Melbourne Property Owners`
6. `Easy Home Maintenance Tasks That Prevent Bigger Repair Bills`

## Blog post structure

- strong benefit-led headline
- local intro if relevant
- practical scannable advice
- examples / checklist section
- CTA to service page
- FAQ section if useful

## Internal link strategy

Each post should link to:
- at least 1 relevant service page
- contact page
- sometimes 1 relevant city page

## Publishing cadence

Recommended initial cadence:
- 3 starter posts at launch
- 2 posts per month afterward

## Quality rules

- do not write generic AI filler
- keep content helpful and specific
- use local relevance naturally
- avoid keyword stuffing
- end with practical CTA
