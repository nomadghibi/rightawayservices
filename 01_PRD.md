# PRD — Right Away Services LLC Website

## 1. Project overview

Build a professional, conversion-focused, highly localized handyman website for **Right Away Services LLC** at `rightawayservices.com`.

The website should help the business:

- rank locally in Google for handyman and home repair searches
- attract homeowners, landlords, property managers, and small businesses
- generate calls and estimate requests from Palm Bay, Melbourne, and surrounding Space Coast areas
- establish trust through professional presentation, local relevance, and clear service messaging

## 2. Business objective

Create a digital presence that positions Right Away Services LLC as a reliable local handyman company serving the Space Coast, with an emphasis on **Palm Bay**, **Melbourne**, and nearby communities.

## 3. Primary goals

1. Increase inbound phone calls
2. Increase estimate / quote requests
3. Improve local organic search visibility
4. Build authority for “handyman near me” and service-specific searches
5. Make the company look established, responsive, and trustworthy

## 4. Target audience

### Primary
- homeowners in Palm Bay and Melbourne
- busy professionals who need quick household fixes
- landlords and rental property owners
- property managers
- seniors needing help with home maintenance
- small commercial clients needing minor repair work

### Secondary
- real estate agents needing punch-list work
- Airbnb / short-term rental hosts
- out-of-town property owners
- local businesses with small maintenance needs

## 5. Site positioning

### Core promise
Fast, dependable handyman and home repair help for Palm Bay, Melbourne, and the surrounding Space Coast.

### Brand attributes
- responsive
- local
- professional
- trustworthy
- practical
- easy to hire
- clear communication
- quality workmanship

## 6. Key website outcomes

Visitors should quickly understand:

- what the company does
- what service areas are covered
- why the company is trustworthy
- how to call or request an estimate immediately

## 7. Functional requirements

### Must-have pages
- Home
- About
- Services overview
- Individual service pages
- Service Areas overview
- Individual area pages
- Contact / Request Estimate
- FAQ
- Blog
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service

### Must-have features
- mobile-first responsive design
- sticky header with call button
- click-to-call on mobile
- contact / estimate form
- service cards
- testimonials section
- local service area section
- FAQ accordions
- Google Maps embed or local trust/location block
- SEO metadata per page
- schema markup
- XML sitemap
- robots configuration
- image optimization
- strong page speed performance

### Core SEO requirements
- heavily localized SEO for Space Coast of Florida

  - Palm Bay

- page-specific title tags and meta descriptions

- FAQ schema
- LocalBusiness schema

- locally relevant headings and copy
- localized CTAs

## 9. Content requirements

### Messaging priorities
- local service
- speed / responsiveness
- professionalism
- quality of work
- ease of booking
- trust and reliability

### Content rules
- no thin content
- no duplicate city pages
- no keyword stuffing
- write in natural language for real homeowners
- each service page must explain:
  - what the service is
  - common customer problems
  - what Right Away Services LLC can help with
  - who it is for
  - where it is offered
  - CTA to call / request estimate

## 10. UX requirements

### UX goals
- fast path to contact
- low cognitive load
- easy navigation
- local trust signals above the fold

### Key UX elements
- prominent phone button
- short estimate form
- CTA repeated multiple times
- badges like:
  - locally serving Space Coast
  - fast response
  - reliable handyman service
- sections with clean scannable layout
