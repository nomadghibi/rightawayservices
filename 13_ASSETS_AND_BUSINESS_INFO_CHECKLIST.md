Use this checklist before launch.

## Business identity
- legal business name
- preferred display name
- phone number
- email address
- website domain
- logo files (SVG/PNG)
- favicon
- business description

## Contact and trust
- hours of operation
- service radius
- physical address or service-area-business details
- license information if applicable
- insurance information if applicable
- warranty / guarantee details if offered

## Services

- service exclusions
- minimum job size if any
- emergency / same-day availability if true

- estimate policy
