# Local SEO Off-Site Plan

Code improves relevance and crawlability, but local visibility also depends on accurate business information, reviews, citations, local relationships, and credible links.

## 1. Google Business Profile

- Use the exact business name, phone, website, hours, and service areas shown on the website.
- Select the most accurate primary category and add only services actually offered.
- Add concise descriptions for handyman, drywall, door repair, painting touch-ups, property maintenance, rental repairs, and vacation-rental maintenance.
- Upload original project photos regularly with customer permission.
- Add service-area and contact updates when business facts change.
- Review performance monthly for Palm Bay, Melbourne, Satellite Beach, and Rockledge queries.

## 2. NAP Consistency

- Maintain one canonical business name, phone, website URL, and address/service-area presentation.
- Audit HomeAdvisor, Facebook, Bing Places, Apple Business Connect, Yelp, Chamber listings, and credible Florida/Brevard directories.
- Correct duplicates rather than creating additional profiles.

## 3. Review Acquisition

- Ask customers shortly after completed work using a direct Google review link.
- Request honest feedback without incentives or suggested star ratings.
- Encourage useful context such as service type and city, but never script a keyword-stuffed review.
- Respond professionally to every review without exposing private project details.

## 4. Local Partnerships and Links

- Contact HOA and condo associations about documented vendor requirements.
- Introduce the company to property-management firms and vacation-rental managers.
- Build referral relationships with Realtors who need pre-listing and turnover punch lists.
- Join credible local Chamber or business organizations where membership provides real community participation.
- Consider local sponsorships only when the organization is legitimate and the relationship is disclosed naturally.

## 5. Outreach Sequence

1. Research the organization using public business information.
2. Confirm the company genuinely serves the organization’s city and property type.
3. Send a short introduction focused on repair-list support and service boundaries.
4. Ask about insurance, licensing, vendor registration, and billing requirements.
5. Follow up once after 7–14 days; do not spam.
6. Record the result and next action in the tracker.
