# Content Brief and Copy Guide — Right Away Services LLC

## Brand voice

The writing should sound:
- local
- capable
- practical
- reassuring
- direct
- easy to understand

Avoid sounding:
- overly corporate
- robotic
- salesy
- spammy
- vague

## Messaging pillars

1. Reliable local help
2. Fast response
3. Professional workmanship
4. Honest communication
5. Convenient service for homeowners and property managers

## Homepage copy priorities

The homepage should answer these questions immediately:
- What does Right Away Services LLC do?
- Where do they work?
- Why should I trust them?
- How do I contact them right now?

## Homepage hero formula

### Headline
Clear benefit + local relevance

Example:
`Reliable Handyman Services for Palm Bay, Melbourne & the Space Coast`

### Subheadline
Who it helps + what kinds of jobs + CTA support

Example:
`From small home repairs to installation and punch-list work, Right Away Services LLC helps homeowners and property managers get jobs done quickly and professionally.`

### CTA buttons
- Call Now
- Request an Estimate

## Service page copy structure

1. Localized headline
2. Short summary of the service
3. Common reasons customers need this service
4. What the company can help with
5. Why choose Right Away Services LLC
6. FAQs
7. CTA

## Area page copy structure

1. City-specific headline
2. Intro paragraph about serving that community
3. Typical customer needs in that area
4. Featured services for that city
5. Why local customers choose the company
6. FAQs for that area
7. CTA

## About page copy goals

The About page should build trust through:
- local presence
- commitment to responsiveness
- professionalism
- quality work
- respectful service
- clear communication

## Contact page copy goals

The Contact page should:
- reduce friction
- make calling feel easy
- make form fill feel easy
- restate service areas
- restate response expectations

## CTA copy examples

- Call Right Away
- Request Your Estimate
- Get Reliable Help Today
- Talk With a Local Handyman
- Book Your Repair Service

## Trust copy examples

- Serving homeowners across Palm Bay, Melbourne, and nearby Space Coast communities
- Reliable help for repairs, installations, and small home improvement projects
- Professional service with fast, clear communication
- Practical solutions for homes, rentals, and small commercial needs

## FAQ topics to cover

- what jobs are offered
- which areas are served
- how estimates work
- whether materials are included
- whether small jobs are accepted
- how quickly service can be scheduled
- whether property managers / landlords are welcome
- whether weekend appointments are available if true

## Conversion rules

- every major page must have CTA near top and bottom
- forms should be short
- phone should be visible
- avoid overwhelming users with too much text before first CTA
- keep paragraphs short and scannable
