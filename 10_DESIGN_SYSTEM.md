# Design System — Right Away Services LLC

## Design goal

Create a professional local-service brand presence that feels:
- trustworthy
- polished
- practical
- local
- conversion-focused

## Style direction

Think:
- modern service business
- simple, premium enough to inspire trust
- clean spacing
- bold clear CTAs
- not trendy for the sake of trendiness

## Visual personality

- dependable
- approachable
- professional
- strong but not aggressive
- homeowner-friendly

## Color direction

Recommended starting palette:

### Primary
- Deep Navy: `#16324F`

### Secondary
- Service Blue: `#245C8F`

### Accent
- Warm Orange: `#F28C28`

### Neutral Dark
- Charcoal: `#1F2937`

### Neutral Light
- Off White: `#F8FAFC`

### Supporting Gray
- Slate Gray: `#64748B`

Notes:
- Use blue tones for trust and professionalism
- Use orange sparingly for CTA emphasis
- Keep background clean and bright

## Typography

Recommended:
- Headings: Inter, Manrope, or Plus Jakarta Sans
- Body: Inter or system sans

Rules:
- large, readable headlines
- generous line-height
- clear hierarchy
- avoid decorative fonts

## UI components to standardize

- primary button
- secondary button
- service card
- area card
- testimonial card
- FAQ item
- section heading
- trust badge
- contact form input
- CTA band
- footer column

## Spacing system

Use a consistent scale such as:
- 4
- 8
- 12
- 16
- 24
- 32
- 48
- 64
- 96

## Imagery direction

Use:
- real handyman / repair imagery
- local home-service visuals
- tool, repair, fixture, and in-home work images
- before/after photos if available
- clean project shots

Avoid:
- cheesy stock photos
- fake smiling call-center images
- irrelevant corporate office images

## Hero section requirements

- clear local headline
- short value proposition
- 1–2 CTAs
- optional supporting image or project photo
- trust line under CTA

## Card design

Cards should be:
- rounded
- clean
- lightly bordered or shadowed
- roomy
- readable
- easy to scan on mobile

## Motion

Keep motion subtle:
- slight hover elevation
- gentle section reveal only if lightweight
- no distracting transitions

## Footer design

Footer should reinforce:
- local service coverage
- contact method
- services
- credibility
- legal pages
